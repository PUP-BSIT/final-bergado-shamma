Thoughts about online class

I think the reason why we conducted face to face classes instead of online class is because we value hands on learning experiences and real-time interaction that leads us to deeper understanding.

Having an online class can be convenient to us as we are experiencing a high heat index. We can also save our money and energy consumed for commuting from our houses to PUP Taguig. Also, having an online class will give them more access to necessary materials needed unlike in laboratory (not everyone has an internet connection).
